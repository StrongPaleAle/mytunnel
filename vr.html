of lol/neliness

The web is a fun place. We often wish we were not bored by the surrounding world. Unfortunately, our default devices are not yet advanced enough to completely isolate us from the world. Visual or auditory inputs distract us from our entertainment, but actually, we would like isolation. My Tunnel of lol/neliness tries to solve this dilemma by allowing the proper use of entertainment on the internet. The project was born as a virtual reality web app. Wearing the visor, you will enter a 3D tunnel, projecting yourself on a journey through images automatically fetched from tumblr.com.

However, why limit yourself to virtual reality? Enjoy it from any device! And remember, for the best results, it takes a little madness.
